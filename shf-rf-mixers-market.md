<p><p>The market for SHF RF mixers can be categorized into two main types: passive mixers and active mixers. </p><p>Passive mixers do not require any external power source and operate by using passive components such as diodes, transformers, and baluns. They are simple and cost-effective but may have limited performance in terms of conversion gain and noise figure.</p><p>On the other hand, active mixers employ active components like transistors or integrated circuits, which require an external power supply. They offer improved performance in terms of conversion gain and noise figure, but are more complex and expensive compared to passive mixers.</p><p>Overall, the choice between passive and active mixers depends on the specific requirements and budget of the RF system.</p></p>
