<p><p>In the aerospace industry, reamers are cutting tools used to enlarge existing holes with precision. There are two types of reamers commonly used: Monobloc type and Insert type. Monobloc type reamers are made from a single piece of material and are suitable for operations requiring higher rigidity and accuracy. On the other hand, Insert type reamers consist of a cutter body and replaceable inserts. These are advantageous as the inserts can be easily replaced when worn out or to accommodate different hole sizes, saving costs and reducing downtime.</p></p>
