<p><p>The advanced lead-acid batteries market includes various types of batteries, such as VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead-Acid) battery, flooded battery, and others. VRLA batteries are sealed and maintenance-free, ensuring no leakage of electrolyte. Flooded batteries have a liquid electrolyte, allowing better performance and longer lifespan but require regular maintenance. Other types of lead-acid batteries may include hybrid batteries, which combine features from both VRLA and flooded batteries. These batteries cater to different applications and offer varying advantages, depending on factors such as cost, maintenance requirements, and performance needs.</p></p>
