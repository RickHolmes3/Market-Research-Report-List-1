<p><p>Automated Guided Vehicles (AGVs) require battery chargers to ensure their uninterrupted operation. Contact chargers are designed to connect directly with the AGV's battery, allowing for electrical transfer through physical contact. On the other hand, non-contact chargers employ wireless or inductive charging technologies, eliminating the need for physical connections. Non-contact chargers offer greater convenience and flexibility, as AGVs can simply drive over a charging pad or park in a specific area to initiate the charging process. Both charger types cater to different AGV requirements and offer various benefits, depending on the specific application.</p></p>
