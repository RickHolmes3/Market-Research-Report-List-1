Needlefelt is manufactured by interlocking fibers using a mechanical process. Wet laid media is produced by dispersing fibers in water and then bonding them together. Melt blown is created by extruding molten polymer fibers onto a conveyor belt. Each type of filtration media has its unique properties and is used in different applications to improve air quality by capturing and removing airborne particles.</p></p>
