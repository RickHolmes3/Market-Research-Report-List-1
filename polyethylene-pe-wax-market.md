<p><p>Polyethylene (PE) wax is a type of synthetic wax derived from petroleum. The market for PE wax can be categorized into three types: polymerized PE wax, modified PE wax, and cracked PE wax. Polymerized PE wax is produced through a process called polymerization, resulting in a high melting point and excellent hardness. Modified PE wax is created by altering the chemical structure of PE wax to enhance its performance in various applications. Cracked PE wax is obtained through the thermal cracking of PE resin, resulting in a low molecular weight wax with excellent compatibility and lubrication properties.</p></p>
