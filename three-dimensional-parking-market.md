<p><p>Three-Dimensional Parking (3DP) refers to the utilization of advanced technologies to overcome parking space limitations. PSH denotes Parking Slot Houses, which are dedicated structures used for parking. PJS stands for Parking Joint System, where multiple parking spaces are connected with automated mechanisms. PXD represents Parking Expansion Deck that increases available parking spaces by adding floors. PPY refers to Parking Platform Yard, where vehicles are stacked on top of each other using mechanical systems. PCS stands for Parking Car Storage, involving automated storage and retrieval of vehicles. PCX symbolizes Parking Car E-exchange, enabling automated vehicle swapping. PDX refers to Parking Dynamic-X, which efficiently manages vehicles in multiple directions. PSX signifies Parking Station X, a centralized parking facility with advanced features. Lastly, PQX denotes Parking Quick Exchange, providing rapid vehicle exchange capabilities.</p></p>
