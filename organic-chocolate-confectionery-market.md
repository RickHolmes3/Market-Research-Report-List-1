<p><p>The organic chocolate confectionery market comprises three main types: milk chocolate, dark chocolate, and white chocolate. Dark chocolate has a higher cocoa content, offering a richer and slightly bitter flavor. It is often preferred by health-conscious consumers due to its potential health benefits. White chocolate, on the other hand, contains cocoa butter but no cocoa solids, resulting in a sweet and creamy taste. These different types cater to varying consumer preferences and dietary requirements.</p></p>
