<p><p>Perforated packaging films are plastic films that are punctured with tiny holes in a controlled pattern. These films are commonly used in the food and beverage industry to improve the shelf life and freshness of perishable products such as fruits, vegetables, bakery items, and meat. Needle perforation, on the other hand, utilizes sharp needles to puncture the film, allowing for controlled airflow and moisture control.
