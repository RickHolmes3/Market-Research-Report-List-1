<p><p>SaaS-based Business Intelligence (BI) market consists of four types: private cloud, public cloud, community cloud, and hybrid cloud. In the private cloud market, BI solutions are hosted on a private network, offering exclusive access and enhanced security to a single organization. Public cloud BI involves hosting services on a shared network, accessible to multiple users through the internet. In the community cloud market, BI is shared among multiple organizations with shared interests, such as industry-specific requirements. Hybrid cloud BI combines elements of private and public clouds, allowing organizations to store data on-premises while leveraging the scalability of the public cloud for analytics.</p></p>
